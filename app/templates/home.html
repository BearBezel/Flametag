Tag your lighter. Stop the “lighter thief” problem.

FlameTag links a QR sticker to a lighter page. The public message shows who it belongs to. The owner-only message unlocks with a PIN.

How it works

1) Stick a FlameTag QR on your lighter
2) Scan → see the public message
3) PIN unlocks the owner-only message

Have a FlameTag code?

Type the code from the sticker to view the message.

Example code: R329QRY8

Admin tools are private at /admin.
