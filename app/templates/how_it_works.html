How FlameTag works

FlameTag helps lost items return to their owners with a simple QR code.

What is FlameTag?
FlameTag is a smart QR sticker system for lost items. Attach a FlameTag sticker to your item. If someone finds it, they scan the QR code and can message the owner instantly. No app required.
How it works
1. Attach your FlameTag
Put the QR sticker on your keys, bag, luggage, bike, wallet, or other item.
2. Someone finds your item
The finder scans the QR code with their phone camera.
3. They send you a message
They can contact you through FlameTag without needing to download an app.
4. You arrange the return
You receive the message and arrange how to get your item back.
Perfect for
Keys • Bags • Luggage • Wallets • Bikes • Cameras • School items • Work equipment
Why people use FlameTag
• No app required • Fast and simple • Anonymous messaging • Works with any phone camera • Easy to attach to everyday items
